Enter your display name:

You start with 100 credits and bet 100 by default

The goal of the game is to score 21 or at least above the dealer
if you score above 21, you lose.

All regular cards are counted by their numbers
Kings, Queens and Jokers are worth 10
Ace = 11
if you have an Ace and your score is above 21, the Ace counts as 1

By clicking hit you are choosing to draw another unknown card from the deck

By clicking stay you are choosing end the game, see both scores, and see who wins

Double or Nothing option will appear when you win, click it to bet double your score, but lose everything if you lose
